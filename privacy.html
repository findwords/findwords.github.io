Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

At Find Words, accessible from findwords.github.io, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that are collected and recorded by Find Words and how we use it.

Because Find Words is a client-side application that processes random word generation live inside your browser, we do not require user accounts, email sign-ups, or standard profile registration to use our services.

1. Local Browser Storage

Find Words utilizes your browser's local storage (such as localStorage) to handle optional user-driven state features, including:

  • Your list of starred/saved vocabulary words.
  • Your generation filter configurations (length, difficulty, starting letter).
  • Audio/sound playback preference states.

This data stays completely inside your local computer or mobile software stack. It is never transmitted back to an external web host or corporate server platform.

2. Log Files

Find Words follows a standard procedure of using log files via static file hosting services. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected by log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. These are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable. The purpose of the information is for analyzing trends, administering the site, tracking users' movement on the website, and gathering demographic information.

3. Third-Party Analytics and Tools

We may leverage lightweight third-party analytics scripts or content delivery architectures to monitor site system uptime and performance metrics. These tools might capture standard technical device indicators. You can elect to decline telemetry or adjust tracking settings within your own privacy browser controls.

4. Children's Information

Another part of our priority is adding protection for children while using the internet. We encourage parents and guardians to observe, participate in, and/or monitor and guide their online activity.

Find Words does not knowingly collect any Personal Identifiable Information from children under the age of 13. If you think that your child provided this kind of information on our website, we strongly encourage you to contact us immediately and we will do our best efforts to promptly remove such information from our records.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, we advise you to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately, after they are posted on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to reach out to us through our repository project channels or via direct site support links.
